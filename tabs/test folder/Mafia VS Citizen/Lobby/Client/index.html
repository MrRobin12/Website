Born on an alien planet, Superman is is rocketed to Earth by his scientist father moments before the planet's destruction. The rocket lands on Earth, where he is found by passing motorists who adopt him and give him the name Clark Kent.

As Clark reaches maturity, he learns he has superhuman abilities which he resolves to use selflessly to help others, fighting everything from petty crime to universal threats. Of all popular culture superheroes, Superman is probably the best known and the most pure of heart and purpose.
